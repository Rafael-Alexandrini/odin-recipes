Broccoli Alfredo Pasta

Photo of Broccoli Alfredo Pasta.

In this recipe we will make the wonderful Broccoli Alfredo Pasta, according to Allrecipes.com.

The Broccoli Alfredo Pasta was named after my childhood friend, Alfredo Al Dente.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces farfalle pasta
  • 2 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg, or to taste
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • fresh parsley sprigs for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook farfalle pasta at a boil, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
  3. Remove to a colander; reserve the pasta water for later use.
  4. Add broccoli to a microwave-safe container along with 1/4 cup water.
  5. Cover and cook on High until bright green and tender with a bite, about 4 minutes.
  6. Drain; season with salt. Keep warm.
  7. Meanwhile, melt butter in a skillet or saucepan over low heat.
  8. Stir in cream and allow to simmer, stirring often, 3 to 4 minutes.
  9. Add cheese and garlic, stirring continuously, until cheese is melted.
  10. Stir in nutmeg and remove from heat.
  11. If the sauce is too thick, stir in a bit of reserved pasta water.
  12. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  13. To serve, combine cooked pasta, cooked broccoli, and sauce.
  14. Garnish with fresh parsley sprigs, if desired.

Conclusion

You now have a Broccoli Alfredo Pasta of your own. Enjoy it while it lasts... ;)
